Our Mission

We edit.
We give.

EditPlug is built with purpose. From every transaction processed through our platform, EditPlug contributes 20% to charity — this comes from us, not from the editor's earnings.

Both editors and creators have an equal say in which charity receives the contribution. 50/50 — your match, your choice.

"Real growth starts with real partnerships — and real partnerships give back."
💜

Shared Decision

Both creator and editor vote on the cause — mental health, education, local communities and more

🌍

20% From EditPlug

Every transaction, EditPlug contributes 20% of our fee to the chosen charity. Your earnings stay yours.
